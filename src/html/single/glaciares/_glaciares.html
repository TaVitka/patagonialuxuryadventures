Los Glaciares National Park, Argentina

Overview

  • Home to the iconic Perito Moreno Glacier, Los Glaciares National Park is a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its stunning glaciers, rugged mountains, and pristine lakes. Trek to the base of Perito Moreno Glacier, hike the Fitz Roy trail, or embark on a boat tour of Lago Argentino to witness the park's natural beauty up close.
