Indigenous Heritage Expedition

Duration

  • 3 days/2 nights

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival and Cultural Immersion

    Arrival at designated meeting point and transfer to the indigenous community.

    Welcome ceremony and introduction to indigenous culture and traditions.

    Participate in traditional activities such as weaving, pottery making, or storytelling.

  • Day 2: Exploration of Sacred Sites

    Visit ancient ceremonial sites and sacred landmarks with indigenous guides.

    Learn about the spiritual significance and history behind each location.

    Engage in cultural exchanges and conversations with community members.

  • Day 3: Farewell and Departure

    Morning cultural exchange and farewell ceremony.

    Optional souvenir shopping at local artisan markets.

    Transfer back to departure point.

Inclusions

  • Accommodations in a local lodge or community-owned guesthouse.
  • Meals as specified in the itinerary (breakfast, lunch, and dinner).
  • Transportation to and from the indigenous community.
  • Guided activities and cultural experiences.

Pricing

  • Starting from $800 per person
