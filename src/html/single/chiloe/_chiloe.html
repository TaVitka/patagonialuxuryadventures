Chiloé Island, Chile

Overview

  • Chiloé Island is a mystical archipelago off the coast of Chile, known for its unique culture, traditional wooden churches, and stunning landscapes. Explore charming fishing villages, visit UNESCO World Heritage sites, and discover the island's rich folklore and mythology. Enjoy fresh seafood, artisanal crafts, and breathtaking views of the Pacific Ocean.
