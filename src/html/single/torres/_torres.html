Torres del Paine National Park, Chile

Overview

  • Torres del Paine is renowned for its towering granite peaks, shimmering glacial lakes, and pristine wilderness. Explore iconic landmarks such as the Torres del Paine Towers, Grey Glacier, and French Valley while hiking along scenic trails. Wildlife encounters with guanacos, foxes, and condors are common in this UNESCO Biosphere Reserve.
