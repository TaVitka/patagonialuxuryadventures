Nahuel Huapi National Park, Argentina

Overview

  • Spanning the provinces of Neuquén and Río Negro, Nahuel Huapi National Park is a haven for outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers. Explore the pristine forests, crystal-clear lakes, and snow-capped peaks of this stunning wilderness area. Hike to scenic viewpoints, kayak on Lake Nahuel Huapi, or spot native wildlife such as Andean condors and deer.
