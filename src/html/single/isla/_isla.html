Isla Navarino, Chile

Overview

  • Isla Navarino is the southernmost inhabited island in Chilean Patagonia, offering remote wilderness and unparalleled natural beauty. Explore rugged coastlines, hike through ancient forests, and witness stunning views of the Beagle Channel and surrounding mountains. Experience the tranquility of the end of the world while immersing yourself in the island's pristine wilderness.
